# Contract Management

## Description
Business domain for contract classification, term, renewal behavior, and coverage attributes used in booking analysis.

## Business Purpose
This domain supports semantic analysis of commercial agreement structure, renewal behavior, and support coverage so that bookings can be interpreted in relation to contractual obligations and lifecycle behavior.
