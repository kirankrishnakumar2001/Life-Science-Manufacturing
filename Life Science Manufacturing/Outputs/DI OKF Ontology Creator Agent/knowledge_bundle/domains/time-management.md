# Time Management

## Description
Business domain for calendar and fiscal reporting periods used in booking and revenue analysis.

## Business Purpose
This domain supports temporal analysis across calendar dates, fiscal years, quarters, and reporting period sequences to enable trend, period-over-period, and fiscal performance reporting.
