# Revenue Analytics

## Description
Business domain for booking transaction measures, pricing, discounting, and contract value analysis.

## Business Purpose
This domain provides the financial and quantitative perspective for bookings, including quantities, list prices, discounts, booking amounts, annual contract value, and total contract value.
